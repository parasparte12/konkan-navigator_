🌊 Who We Are

We Are Konkan Lovers

Konkan Navigator was born in 2020 when a group of friends from Mumbai took their first real road trip down the coastal highway. We were stunned — hidden beaches with zero crowds, ancient sea forts, friendly fishing villages, and food that we still dream about.

We realized there was no platform that truly captured the magic of Konkan. Travel apps showed only the famous spots. The real Konkan — the secret waterfalls, the local food joints, the off-road paths to viewpoints — was invisible online.

So we built Konkan Navigator. A platform by explorers, for explorers. We partner with local guides, document hidden spots, and create tools to help every type of traveler plan their perfect Konkan journey.
