💡 Konkan Travel Tips

Expert advice for the best Konkan experience

🌦️
Best Season: Oct–Feb
Post-monsoon brings greenery; winter is perfect for beaches and forts.
🚗
Getting Around
Rent a bike or car for maximum flexibility. State buses connect major towns.
💵
Budget: ₹1500–3000/day
Homestays are affordable and give authentic local experiences.
📱
Connectivity
Download offline maps. Some coastal areas have limited network coverage.
🍽️
Must-Try Foods
Sol kadhi, Malvani fish curry, kombdi wade, and fresh coconut water at every stop.
